Bronze Age Perverts Carrot Salad Of Power

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 big carrot shredded lengthwise
  • virgin coconut oil
  • apple cider vinegar
  • liquid honey
  • salt, pepper and garlic to taste

Steps

  1. clean carrot & shred carrot
  2. liquify virgin coconut oil
  3. mix together with shredded carrot
  4. pour apple cider vinegar & liquid honey
  5. add salt, pepper & garlic to taste
  6. enjoy
